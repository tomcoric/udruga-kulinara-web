About Us

Đakovački Kulin Meat Producers Association

It is my pleasure to welcome you on behalf of the "Đakovački Kulin" Meat Producers Association and to invite all interested parties to join the work of our Association.

Through these pages we wish to present the work of the Association in a transparent manner and to introduce you to the activities we carry out for the benefit of our members and the wider community.

For more than thirty years, the quality of Slavonian kulin has been evaluated in Đakovo. The first written record of a kulin competition in Đakovo dates back to 1988, held as part of the "Days of Wine and Kulin" event, under the patronage of the newspaper Večernji list. From 1990 to the present day, the competition has been held as part of the "Đakovački Vezovi" festival.

The Meat Producers Association was founded in 2003 in Đakovo by a group of enthusiasts, producers and lovers of traditional delicacies, with the aim of protecting the authentic, traditional production of cured meat products. The Association was registered as the "Slavonska gastronomija" Meat Producers Association with its headquarters in Đakovo. Mr. Mato Šimunić from Vinkovci was elected the first president, and Mr. Milan Šuša from Osijek was elected secretary. The Association was founded with the aim of uniting as many small producers from the Slavonia region as possible and preserving traditional Slavonian cured meat production.

In 2008, the Association changed its name to "Gastronomski kulin", and since 2013 it has been operating under the name "Đakovački kulin" Meat Producers Association. It now has over sixty members and is one of the largest associations of cured meat producers in Slavonia.

Today the Management Board consists of five members as defined by the Statute, and the Association also has a Supervisory Board, a Disciplinary Committee, and a liquidator.

Association members participate in numerous events in Đakovo and throughout Croatia. They present, nurture and preserve our traditional heritage.

In Đakovo, with the support of the city and the Đakovo Tourist Board, the Association organises three cured meat product evaluations during the year:

Early February — sausage competition (during the Đakovo Carnival)
Around Easter — evaluation of kulin seka (a dry-cured pork product)
Early July — kulin competition (as part of the Đakovački Vezovi festival)
